About Me

I am a passionate learner, classical dancer, and educator who believes in the power of creativity, discipline, and continuous growth. My journey has been shaped by my involvement in performing arts, teaching young learners, and exploring the world of business and entrepreneurship.

I have trained extensively in Bharatanatyam and successfully completed my Arangetram, a milestone that represents years of dedication, discipline, and performance training. Through this journey, I developed strong stage presence, perseverance, and a deep appreciation for Indian classical art forms.

Alongside Bharatanatyam, I am currently practicing Kathak, which has helped me further expand my understanding of rhythm, storytelling, and expression within Indian classical dance traditions.

Dance has given me the opportunity to perform on numerous stages and represent India at international cultural events in Turkey and Hungary. I have participated in over 30 group stage performances and have had the honor of performing in front of Former Chief Minister of Gujarat, Vijay Rupani.

Alongside my artistic pursuits, I currently work as a Playgroup Teacher at Little Millennium School, where I create engaging and supportive learning environments for young children. This role has strengthened my communication skills, patience, adaptability, and leadership abilities.

Academically, I am pursuing a BBA in Digital Business and Entrepreneurship from IIM Bangalore, where I am developing knowledge in business strategy, marketing, and innovation.

Through my experiences in performing arts, education, and business studies, I continue to build a well-rounded perspective that values creativity, cultural expression, and lifelong learning.
